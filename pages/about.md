## About Me

<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
I am an <strong>AI & LLM Engineer</strong> specialising in large language models, RAG systems, AI agents, and NLP — with a background spanning <strong>physics research</strong>, <strong>computational modelling</strong>, and <strong>end-to-end AI development</strong>. I currently work at <strong>ORCALYS</strong>, where I design and build production AI systems for industrial automation and enterprise knowledge management. My work spans the full development lifecycle: from retrieval pipeline architecture and LLM evaluation to deployment on cloud and HPC infrastructure.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
My engineering work is grounded in rigorous research. During my <strong>Master of Digital Text Analysis</strong> at the University of Antwerp, I developed large-scale benchmarking frameworks to evaluate retrieval effectiveness and context utilisation in long-context LLMs — work centred on <strong>Needle-in-a-Haystack evaluation</strong> using synthetic data pipelines. Prior to that, a <strong>Ph.D. and postdoc in Physics</strong> gave me deep experience in numerical simulation, data analysis, and computational modelling — habits of precision I carry directly into AI system design.
</p>

<div style="text-align: justify; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
  <p style="line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1rem;">
  My core areas of expertise include:
  </p>
  <ul style="line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.5rem; text-align: justify;">
    <li><strong>Enterprise RAG & Graph RAG</strong>: End-to-end retrieval systems with vector databases (FAISS, Chroma), semantic search, and benchmark-driven evaluation</li>
    <li><strong>AI Agents & Agentic Workflows</strong>: Designing and orchestrating multi-step agents for automation and knowledge access</li>
    <li><strong>LLM Evaluation</strong>: Retrieval benchmarking, synthetic dataset generation, and context utilisation assessment at scale</li>
    <li><strong>NLP Pipelines</strong>: Text classification, topic modelling, terminology extraction, and document analytics across multiple domains</li>
    <li><strong>Infrastructure & Deployment</strong>: Docker, Ray, HPC clusters, distributed training, and scalable inference for production AI</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.8;">
I have applied these skills across <strong>healthcare</strong> (medical NLP and LLM fine-tuning at KdG University), <strong>industrial automation</strong> (enterprise AI at ORCALYS), and <strong>digital humanities</strong> (computational text analysis and machine-generated content detection). I bring the analytical depth of a physicist and the practical focus of an engineer to every system I build.
</p>
