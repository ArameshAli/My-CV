I am an **AI & LLM Engineer** based in Antwerp, Belgium, specialising in large language models, 
RAG systems, AI agents, and NLP. I currently work at **ORCALYS**, where I design and build 
production AI systems for industrial automation and enterprise knowledge management — from 
retrieval pipeline architecture and agentic workflow automation to deployment on cloud and HPC 
infrastructure.

My work is grounded in rigorous research. I hold a **Ph.D. in Physics** and a **Master of 
Digital Text Analysis** from the University of Antwerp, where my thesis focused on evaluating 
retrieval effectiveness and context utilisation in long-context LLMs using large-scale 
Needle-in-a-Haystack benchmarking frameworks.

I have applied AI across **healthcare** (medical NLP and LLM fine-tuning), **industrial 
automation** (enterprise RAG at ORCALYS), and **digital humanities** (text classification and 
machine-generated content detection). I bring the analytical depth of a physicist and the 
practical focus of an engineer to every system I build.
